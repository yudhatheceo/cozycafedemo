Who We Are

A Neighborhood Café
with Heart

Cozy Cafe was born from a simple idea: everyone deserves a place where they feel welcome, comfortable, and at home — with an amazing cup of coffee in hand.

Nestled in Cadillac, Michigan, we've become a gathering spot for students burning through late-night study sessions, professionals looking for a quiet workspace, families catching up over brunch, and everyone in between.

We believe great coffee should come with great company — and a really comfortable chair.
